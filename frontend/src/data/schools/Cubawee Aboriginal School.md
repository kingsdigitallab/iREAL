**Role of the Department and relationship to the APB**

Teacher Ella Hiscocks to Department of Education -- re: salary rebate --
"In the November Gazette (Public Services Salaries Act (No 2) 1931) it
stated that where an officers salary, who is a widow, or a widower does
not exceed three hundred pounds per annum, they were entitled to a
rebate of fifteen pounds per annum and to make application for same. I
made application, accompanied by declaration on the 23^rd^ November but
have heard nothing since. Do you know of any reason why I would not be
entitled to it and if others have received payment."

10/8/1932 -- Teacher Ella Hiscocks -- Application for cleaning allowance
-- "I wish to make application for a cleaning allowance for above
school, in the past children and I did it, but the girls attending now
are small and have been so ill of late, that I cannot expect them to do
it and will have to get one of the women from the Station to come. The
building is so old, that it is hard to keep it clean unless done often.
-- note from Chief Inspector -- This school is being conducted upon a
verandah at the post office building. As the only cleaning possible is
sweeping the small area used and dusting the desks, I do not consider a
cleaning allowance is justified."

2/5/1935 -- Teacher Ella Campbell Hiscocks -- Closing of the School on
the 18^th^ and 24^th^ April -- "I wish to advise that owing to the AP
Boards instructions to take one of my pupils to a situation at Pilliga
in the N. West, I was unable to open school on the 18^th^ and 24^th^
instant, as I left here on the 18^th^ and returned on the 24^th^, being
the shortest possible time to do the journey in."

**Teachers**

19/10/1931 -- Application for Forage Allowance -- driving in her own car
to and from the school. Special features listed as -- Residence nearest
available. Mrs. Hiscocks is a widow and gives sewing instruction
herself. Residence is 4 1/2miles from the school.

27/11/1931 -- Mrs Ella Hiscocks -- Re: Travelling allowance -- The
Education Department granting traveling allowance for traveling to and
from Cubawee school.

28/7/1933 -- Teacher Ella Hiscocks -- Application for sanction to
closing of Cubawee school -- "I wish to apply for your sanction to the

4/12/1934 -- Teacher Ella Campbell -- Hiscocks -- Closure of School --
"I wish to advise that I was unable to attend school on Monday and
Tuesday 26^th^ and 27^th^ Instant owing to my sons illness, I was not
